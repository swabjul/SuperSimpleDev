Challenge Exercises

We'll use JavaScript to convert temperature from Celcius (°C) to Fahrenheit (°F). The formula is:

  • Fahrenheit = (Celsius * 9/5) + 32
  • Celcius = (Fahrenheit - 32) * 5/9

2l. The temperature is 25°C. Calculate temperature in Fahrenheit. (77)

          (25 * 9/5) + 32

          //77

2m. The temperature is 86°F. Calculate the temperature in Celcius. (30)

          (86 - 32) * 5/9

          // 30

2n. The temperature is -5°C. Calculate the temperature in Fahrenheit. (23)

          (-5 * 9/5) + 32

          //23
